ABOUT ME

Hola! My name is Lucero (Lu) Munoz-Vazquez. I am originally from Hidalgo, Mexico and migrated to the US when I was 4 years old.
I am an artist and creator at heart, with burning interest in tech. I was first exposed to computers in my home and although it was not connected to the internet, I liked exploring the built-in features and settings, as well as the Microsoft application, “Paint”. - That is where my fascination with computers and technology began, and also, where my love for digital art came to fruition.
I am an aspiring web developer with an interest in UX/UI design.
Currently, I work as a developer/programmer for a healthcare research firm where I have been exposed to several different applications and languages (C#, html, js, jQuery).
I would like to continue learning about web development to further my career and expand on my knowledge and skills.
